Small Things Deprecation Notice

Published on July 7th, 2021

Hello, DX Studios here. This announcement is in regards to the deprecation of DX Studios' Small Things engine for Friday Night Funkin'.

What does this mean?

Starting from here on out, all future DX Studios projects will no longer be using the Small Things engine. The reasoning for this is that we will no longer be taking the time to make sure the engine is up to use for our Friday Night Funkin'-related works.

What will we be using instead?

We will be focusing on using Kade Engine in the future, as it's proven to be a much more reliable engine.

Why are you making this change?

The two founders of DX Studios no longer have the time between the many projects we work on to keep up with what we feel is an aging engine. In our eyes, there's much better things, such as Kade Engine, that we can use to make our projects on.

- DX Studios
